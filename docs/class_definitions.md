1. Pothole:
   Circular or irregular cavity in the road surface caused by wear or erosion.

2. Crack:
   Linear fracture on the road surface without significant depth.

3. Open Manhole:
   Exposed or partially covered manhole opening on roads.

4. Waterlogged Road:
   Road surface submerged or partially submerged due to rain or drainage failure.

5. Broken Road Edge / Shoulder Damage:
   Erosion or collapse along the edges of the road.

6. Construction / Repair Zone:
   Road segments under repair with loose gravel, barricades, or dug surfaces.
